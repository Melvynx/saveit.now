# Product

## Register

product

## Users

SaveIt.now is used by people who collect web pages, videos, PDFs, products, and other references across devices. They are usually trying to save, search, review, share, or automate access to their bookmarks without managing folders manually.

## Product Purpose

SaveIt.now is a bookmark management SaaS for saving links, enriching them with metadata and AI processing, and accessing them through the web app, browser extensions, mobile surfaces, CLI, and API. Success means users can trust the product to keep saved material organized, searchable, and easy to reuse.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, practical, precise. The product should feel like a reliable workspace for stored knowledge, with clear affordances and low visual noise.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboard clutter, oversized marketing-style account pages, novelty navigation patterns, and generic SaaS gradients. Authenticated screens should not feel like landing pages.

## Design Principles

- Put the workflow first: screens should help users finish account, bookmark, and API tasks with minimal scanning cost.
- Keep actions predictable: standard controls, clear labels, and restrained states beat invented UI.
- Preserve trust: destructive, billing, sharing, and API actions need explicit context and calm visual treatment.
- Use the existing shadcn token system as the source of truth for color, spacing, and component behavior.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible controls. Motion should be limited to state feedback and respect reduced-motion preferences.
